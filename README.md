# Brain SM — Social Media Behavior Analysis & Digital Wellbeing Platform

## Overview
**Brain SM** is an experimental web-based platform designed to analyze **social media usage patterns** and generate meaningful insights about user behavior, engagement trends, and digital habits.

The project explores how **AI, machine learning, and data analytics** can be applied to transform raw social media activity into **structured reports, visual dashboards, and awareness-driven insights**, promoting more mindful and informed digital usage.

---

## Problem Statement
Social media plays a central role in modern life, yet users often lack visibility into how their online behavior affects focus, productivity, and mental well-being.

There is a growing need for systems that:
- Analyze social media usage patterns
- Highlight excessive engagement or distraction trends
- Present insights in a clear, visual, and understandable manner
- Support awareness rather than judgment or diagnosis

---

## Solution
Brain SM provides a data-driven approach to understanding social media behavior by:
- Analyzing daily social media activity signals
- Generating personalized reports and activity summaries
- Visualizing trends through interactive dashboards
- Sending alerts and reminders to encourage healthier digital habits

The platform is designed as a **learning-focused prototype**, emphasizing ethical AI usage, transparency, and interpretability of behavioral data.

---

## Key Features
- Analysis of daily social media activity patterns  
- Personalized behavioral and activity reports  
- Visual dashboards for easy interpretation of insights  
- Activity scoring and trend summaries over time  
- Email notifications and alerts via SMTP  
- Suggestion-based feedback to help reduce distractions  

---

## Potential Applications & Future Scope
With further development and ethical safeguards, Brain SM can be extended to support:
- **Awareness of excessive social media usage and digital addiction patterns**
- **Identification of prolonged exposure to inappropriate or adult content trends** (at an analytical level)
- **Digital wellbeing and productivity support tools**
- **Academic and psychological research**, enabling researchers to study anonymized behavioral patterns and digital engagement trends

These applications would focus on **insight generation and awareness**, not clinical diagnosis or intervention.

---

## System Architecture (High-Level)
1. User activity data is collected and preprocessed  
2. Machine learning models analyze behavioral patterns  
3. Processed data is stored in structured databases  
4. Kibana dashboards visualize trends and activity metrics  
5. Email alerts notify users with reports and reminders  

---

## Technologies Used
- **Python** — Core data processing and ML workflows  
- **Natural Language Processing** — Behavioral pattern analysis  
- **Hugging Face** — Experimentation with NLP and AI models  
- **Kibana** — Data visualization and monitoring dashboards  
- **PostgreSQL** — Structured data storage  
- **SMTP** — Email notifications and alerts  
- **Docker** — Containerization and environment consistency  
- **HTML/CSS** — Frontend interface  
- **Git & GitHub** — Version control and collaboration  
- **VS Code** — Development environment
